❓ FAQ
When do I get paid?
Commissions are processed weekly. Payments are released every Monday for the previous week's earnings. Minimum withdrawal is 10 USDT.
How do I upgrade my commission tier?
Tiers are upgraded automatically based on your cumulative sign-up count. Reaching 601 sign-ups puts you at Platinum (38%). Diamond tier requires 900+ sign-ups.
Can I refer sub-affiliates?
Yes! You earn 0–10% on every trade your sub-affiliates' referrals make. Go to Promotion Tools to get your sub-affiliate invite link.
What counts as a First Time Deposit (FTD)?
An FTD is recorded when a user you referred makes their first deposit of at least $10 USDT equivalent after completing identity verification.
